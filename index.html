Core Web3 Concepts

🔗 Decentralization

Decentralization removes control from a single authority and distributes it across a network. No central point of failure, no censorship, and full user ownership of data and assets.

⚡ Arbitrum L2

Arbitrum is a Layer 2 scaling solution for Ethereum. It processes transactions off-chain and posts compressed data to Ethereum mainnet, achieving 10-40x lower fees while maintaining security.

📜 Smart Contracts

Self-executing programs that run on blockchains. They automatically enforce agreements when conditions are met, powering DeFi, NFTs, DAOs, and countless decentralized applications.

Why Arbitrum Matters

🐌 Why Ethereum Needed Layer 2

Ethereum mainnet can only process ~15 transactions per second. During high demand, gas fees spike to $50-$100+ per transaction. Layer 2 solutions like Arbitrum solve this scalability crisis.

🚀 What Arbitrum Is

Arbitrum uses Optimistic Rollup technology. It "rolls up" hundreds of transactions into a single batch, posts them to Ethereum, and inherits Ethereum's security while processing transactions faster and cheaper.

💡 Real-World Benefit

On Arbitrum, a swap that costs $50 on Ethereum mainnet costs under $1. Same security, same Ethereum ecosystem — just faster and affordable for everyday users.
